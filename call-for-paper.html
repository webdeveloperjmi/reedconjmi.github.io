Conference Theme

Recent Advances in Electrical, Electronics & Digital Healthcare Technologies.

International Conference on Recent Advances in Electrical, Electronics & Digital Healthcare Technologies, 2023 (REEDCON2023) is going to be held from May 1 to 3, 2023 at Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi. The conference focuses on original research work and its industry applications intended for the exchange of ideas, research outcomes, and practical experience. Leading experts from Academia and Industry will participate and deliberate on the challenges faced in the respective fields and innovative solutions.

Area to be covered, but not limited to:

Authors are requested to send original papers based on the research work/field experience in the categories listed below:

Track-1: Power System and Power Electronics

Track Chair: Ahteshamul Haque

Electrical machines and drives Systems, Power System resilience, FACT Devices, wide area monitoring & control systems, Intelligent Techniques in power systems, Power System Protection, Advanced metering and protection systems, Wide Area Monitoring and Control systems in the Smartgrid Technologies, Sustainable technologies for smart grids and micro-grids, Grid Codes, Economics of power generation, Renewable energy sources (Solar, Wind, Fuel Cell etc), Grid integration of distributed energy resources. IoT Infrastructure for energy management in smart cities, Energy storage technologies, Electric and hybrid vehicles, Transportation electrification and charging infrastructure, Modelling, Control and stability of Power Electronics based system. FACTS, HVDC and pulsed power converters, Fault Diagnosis in Power Electronic Converters. Hardware in loop based design, Design of Reliability for Power Electronics Converters, Power Electronics Converter topologies for various application, Grid Connected Inverters, Fault Tolerant converters, Lighting and illumination. Applications of power electronics in renewable energy sources.

Track-2: Control & Instrumentation:

Track Chair: Tarikul Islam

Robust, Stochastic and Multivariable Control, Time-Delay Systems, Intelligent and Artificial Intelligence-Based Control, Control Algorithms implementation, Control Applications, Motion Control, Optimal Control, Adaptive Control, Real-Time Systems, Modelling and Simulation, Recent Developments in Measurement Techniques, Sensors, Smart Sensor, Sensor Array, Electronic Instrumentation, Signal Conditioning.

Track-3: Electronics & Communication:

Track Chair: Z A Jaffery

Cognitive Radio & Dynamic Spectrum Management, Mobile and Wireless Technologies, Smart Antennas, Multiple Access Techniques, Image, Audio & Video Processing Techniques, Compression & Coding, Multimedia Tools, Applications & Security.

Track-4: Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things and Cloud Computing:

Track Chair: Shabana Mehfuz

Soft Computing and Application, AI techniques for healthcare, Intelligent Decision-Making Systems, Adaptive Systems, Machine learning and computer vision, Deep learning, Intelligent and knowledge based systems, Natural language processing, IoT Enabling Technologies, IoT Networks & Communications, IoT Services and Intelligence, IoT Systems and Applications, Privacy, security, and social issues relevant to intelligent systems and IoT, Theory, method and technology for next generation cloud computing, Cloud as a service, Cloud Infrastructure, Access Control, Authorization, Authentication in Cloud Computing, Machine learning and Deep learning in Cloud Computing, Cloud computing support for mainstream artificial intelligence framework, Management tools for next generation cloud computing platforms, Cloud Metering and Monitoring, Cloud Operations, Cloud applications.

Track-5: Medical Healthcare Technologies:

Track Chair: Munna Khan

Augmented and virtual reality, Biomedical/ Genomic Signal & Image Processing, , Biomedical Electronics, Wearable & connected devices, standards and protocols for e-Health, Biosensors at the micro/nano-scale, Body area networking, IoT for Smart Healthcare, Telemedicine, Clinical Data Visualisation Standards, Healthcare Information systems, E-Health services/applications for chronic & serious diseases, elderly people, public health, rural areas, Non-invasive brain stimulation, Physical exercise and neuroplasticity, Physical training and cognition, Telemonitoring, Artificial Intelligence for Community Medicine, Wireless Medical Devices, Software as a Medical Device, Exergames in motor skill learning, Motor coordination assessment apps for wheelchair users, Rehabilitation Technologies, Medical Applications of Robots, Musculoskeletal modeling and simulations, Transcranial magnetic stimulation (TMS), Transcranial direct current stimulation (tDCS).

Prospective authors are invited to contribute original research papers, which can be submitted electronically via EDAS. Accepted papers after the review and presentation in the conference will be included in the proceedings of REEDCON2023 at IEEE Xplore Digital Library (www.ieeexplore.org). An extended version of the selected papers may be forwarded to the peer-reviewed Journal for publication.

Jamia Millia Islamia is a Central University with NAAC A++ accreditation. The MHRD's National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) placed Jamia at the 3rd position in the University.
